Image to PDF Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-06-30
App: Image to PDF Converter (com.simpletools.imagetopdf)

1. Introduction

Image to PDF is designed to work fully offline and helps users convert images on their device into PDF files.

This policy explains what information may be processed when using the app.

2. Information We Collect

The app does not require login, account creation, or profile registration.

The app does not run a backend service for user file storage.

The app includes Google AdMob banner ads. AdMob may collect certain device and usage information under Google's own privacy policies.

3. Files and Documents

Images selected for conversion are processed on your device.

Generated PDF documents are stored on your device.

The app does not upload your files or documents to our servers.

4. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob for banner advertising.

Google may process certain information according to its policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from apps and sites

5. Data Security

Your files remain on-device, and device-level protections (such as lock screen and system updates) help protect local data.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age required by applicable local law.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The Last Updated date will be revised when changes are made.
