# Privacy Policy for Image to PDF

Last Updated: 2026-06-30

## 1. Introduction
Image to PDF (Play Store title: Image to PDF Converter, package: com.simpletools.imagetopdf) respects your privacy. This app is designed to work fully offline and to help you convert images on your device into PDF documents.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app and how that information is handled.

## 2. Information We Collect
The app itself does not require account registration, login, or profile creation.

The app does not operate a backend server for user content.

However, the app displays Google AdMob banner advertisements. As an ad provider, Google AdMob may collect certain information from your device or app interactions for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and policy compliance. This processing is governed by Google's own privacy terms and policies.

## 3. Files and Documents
Image to PDF processes images that you select on your device.

Generated PDF files are created and stored on your device.

Your selected images and generated PDF documents remain on your device unless you choose to share or move them using your own device tools.

The app does not upload your documents or files to a cloud service operated by us.

## 4. Advertising
This app uses Google AdMob to display banner ads.

Because AdMob is used, certain device or usage data may be collected by Google or its partners in accordance with their policies. We recommend reviewing:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## 5. Data Security
Since app documents remain on-device, you control access to your files through your device security settings.

We encourage users to keep their devices updated and protected with appropriate security controls (for example, device lock screen and OS updates).

## 6. Children's Privacy
Image to PDF is not directed to children under the age required by applicable local law.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information through third-party ad services, please contact the relevant ad provider directly and contact us using the details below.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, product, or policy changes.

When updates are made, the Last Updated date at the top of this policy will be revised.
